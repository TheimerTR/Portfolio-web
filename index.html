Suits

Suits is a 2D puzzle-platformer where you transform your character into different shapes to overcome challenges. Solve puzzles in a world where sacrifice is the key to progress. Explore a surreal cube world where each face reveals hidden secrets, new challenges, and shifting perspectives.
My work in the game was developing the player movement, the death mechanic, the cube stencil effect and the main menu.

Dreamward

You are a spark of hope, a light in the darknesss, the protector of dreams. In this short but challenging adventure against the representation of trauma of different people, learning their story and fighting the nightmares so they can dream again. You will need to learn the different patterns of bosses and manage your stamina to survive. It even has a one hit mode for those who enjoy difficult challenges. Get over your fears and prepare to protect those dreams.
My work in the game was developing the player movement and combat, the tutorial, the lobby and the enviroment of the levels.
