Suits

Suits is a 2D puzzle-platformer where you transform your character into different shapes to overcome challenges. Solve puzzles in a world where sacrifice is the key to progress. Explore a surreal cube world where each face reveals hidden secrets, new challenges, and shifting perspectives.
My work in the game was developing the player movement, the death mechanic, the cube stencil effect and the main menu.
Link to the page: Itch.io Suits


Dreamward

You are a spark of hope, a light in the darknesss, the protector of dreams. In this short but challenging adventure against the representation of trauma of different people, learning their story and fighting the nightmares so they can dream again. You will need to learn the different patterns of bosses and manage your stamina to survive. It even has a one hit mode for those who enjoy difficult challenges. Get over your fears and prepare to protect those dreams.
My work in the game was developing the player movement and combat, the tutorial, the lobby and the enviroment of the levels.
Link to the page: Itch.io Dreamward


Magic Frogs

Counter your enemies attacks with the power of your croaks and survive the oppressive flies. In this game, you will parry the waves of your enemy by generating the same waves as him in the same order, while dodging flys and searching for healing.
My work in the game was developing the player movement and wave generator.
Link to the page: Itch.io Magic Frogs


Alien Extraction

As mix between an alien and a human, you are in search of discovering what happened in a laboratory after reciving a sign of SOS, there you will fight against different types of Xenomorph. In this Diablo like game, we used the Alien IP from the films and was done by an equip of more than 30 people.
My work in the game was helping in the enviroment art team, doing all types of assets for it.
Link to the page: Alien: Extraction


Engine Bones

Engine Bones is a 3D Game Engine developed as a part of the Videogame Engine subject by Alberto Hidalgo García and Sonia Cristina Ojeda Lanz, students at CITM (UC) University during 3rd year of Game Design and Game Development degree.
My work in the game was developing the Game Objects, the hierarchy, the inspector, the resource manager, the component mesh, the component material, the drag&drop, the component UI, the mouse picking, the asset explorer, the log console and the pause&play.
Link to the page: Engine Bones

Five Nights At Pedro's

Five Nights at Pedro's is a horror puzzle game based on the Five Nights at Freddy's franchise by Scott Cawthon. The game takes place in a closed room with several key points that must be monitored or interacted with to stop the animatronics from attacking you and survive the night.
My work in the game was setting up de enviroment, the vr setup, the programming of bonnie, puppet and some interactuables.
Link to the page: Five Nights At Pedro's
